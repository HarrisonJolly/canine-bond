Services and Prices

I'm dedicated to providing your canine companion with the care they deserve while employing ethical methods and a compassionate approach. Your dog's well-being is paramount in all of my efforts. About Me

Hi, I'm Donna Bond and I am Certified Dog Behaviourist holding a level 5 Diploma in Canine Behaviour & Psychology, a Family Dog Mediator, a Certified Canine Behaviourist member with INTODogs and I am also covered by the UK Dog Behaviour & Training Charter.

In addition to my professional qualifications and accreditations. I am dedicated and committed in continuing ongoing education and courses, keeping my knowledge up to date. At present I am working towards completing a level 3 Canine Nutrition Diploma.
